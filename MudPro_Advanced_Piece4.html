Volumetric Method: When circulation is not possible (plugged string, no DP).
1. Lubricate mud to bottom — add mud on top of influx
2. Wait for mud to fall (influx rises)
3. Bleed gas at surface keeping CASING pressure constant
4. Repeat until influx is out
Key: Allow casing pressure to rise by the LUBRICANT HP equivalent before bleeding.
